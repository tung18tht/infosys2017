\begin{homeworkProblem}[Labwork 9]

\begin{homeworkSection}{Which normal form does the employee database satisfy?}
The employee database satisfies the \textbf{3NF} because:
\begin{itemize}
    \item No repeated columns and only atomic values (1NF)
    \item Non-key columns depend on the primary keys (2NF)
    \item No columns that are transitively dependent on the primary key (3NF)
\end{itemize}
\end{homeworkSection}
